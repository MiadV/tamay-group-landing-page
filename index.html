WELCOME TO TAMAY BUSINESS GROUP

TBG is a privately owned business located in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. We provide retail and wholesale services, from footwear, fashion bags, intelligent consumer goods to wide range of perfume collections. As an aspiring enterprise, we are dedicated to offering the best shopping service at our store outlets. Under its umbrella, Tamay Group runs Tamay Shoes brand outlets, Eden, multi-branded perfume stores, and department stores, Gilas & GilasCo.

WHAT MAKES US SPECIAL?

Confident with our retail expertise, Tamay Group has been transformed from a small business into a medium sized enterprise with a total of twelve store outlets in Malaysia and two store outlets overseas in Hong Kong
We pride ourselves on an efficient service in offering a variety of retail products, and our friendly and courteous customer service

Our mission is to become a leading retail service in Malaysia on the short run and South East Asia on the long run.

Our motto describes us best - Take A Big Step
